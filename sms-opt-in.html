How It Works

01

Text the number. Send any message to (877) 804-4213 from your phone. Example: "Hi" or "What time is practice?"

02

You are opted in. By sending a text, you consent to receive team updates. The bot will reply with team information.

03

Ask anything. Text questions about practice times, game schedules, gear requirements, team rules, or snack sign-ups.

04

Opt out anytime. Reply STOP to unsubscribe from all messages. No questions asked.

What You Will Receive

  • Practice reminders
  • Game-day notifications (opponent, time, location)
  • Snack duty reminders and sign-up confirmations
  • Replies to your questions about team logistics

Important

Message and data rates may apply. Approximately 2-5 messages per week during the season. Reply STOP to cancel. Reply HELP for assistance. This service is for parents and guardians of team players only.
